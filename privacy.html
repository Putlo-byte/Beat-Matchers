Information we collect

Beatmatchers is a static website and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details unless you choose to send it to us (for example, by emailing us). We do not run accounts, logins, or our own analytics database.

Some information is collected automatically by third-party services we use, as described below. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, the pages you view, and the approximate location derived from your IP address.

Cookies and similar technologies

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. We and our third-party partners use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences (such as your cookie-consent choice), and—where permitted—to serve and measure advertising. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can change your consent choice on this site at any time using the “Cookie settings” control.

For more detail, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Your choices and consent

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we ask for your consent before non-essential (advertising and measurement) cookies are set. You can grant, refuse, or withdraw consent at any time using the cookie banner or the “Cookie settings” link. Until consent is given, advertising cookies that require consent are not loaded.

Children’s privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Data retention and security

Because we do not operate our own server-side database, any retention of automatically collected data is governed by the privacy policies of the third-party services listed above. We take reasonable care to use reputable providers, but no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.
